Selling on Etsy & Gumroad

Your products are ready. Here's how to get paid.

Option 1: Etsy (Best for Volume)

  1. Go to etsy.com/sell — create shop (free, 5 minutes)
  2. Click "Add a listing" — upload your generated file as the digital download
  3. Title: use descriptive keywords. Example: "Notion Budget Template | Personal Finance Tracker | Expense Planner"
  4. Price: $3-15. Start low ($5) until you get 10+ reviews, then raise.
  5. Tags: use all 13 slots. Every tag is a search someone might type.
  6. Cost: $0.20 per listing + 6.5% transaction fee. No monthly fee.

Option 2: Gumroad (Simpler, Better Margins)

  1. Go to gumroad.com — create account (free)
  2. Products → New Product → upload your file
  3. Set price. Gumroad charges 10% flat (no listing fee).
  4. Advantage: No listing fee. Better for testing.

Quick Wins for First Sales

  • Reddit: Post in r/Notion, r/Productivity, r/personalfinance, r/resumes
  • Pinterest: Create 5 pins per product. Pinterest is Etsy's #1 traffic source.
  • Bundle: After 3-5 products, bundle at $19.95 (perceived as great value)
  • Seasonality: Budget templates sell in January. Planners in December. Resume templates year-round.
